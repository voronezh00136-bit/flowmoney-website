Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your FlowMoney account and all associated data

⚠ This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes all your data from our servers and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to proceed before requesting deletion.

Option 1 — Delete in the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the FlowMoney app:

  1. Open the FlowMoney app on your device
  2. Tap Profile in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll down and tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to our support team:

✉ Request Account Deletion

Include the email address associated with your account. We process deletion requests within 5 business days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile and account credentials
  • All income and transaction records
  • Budget categories and spending history
  • Financial goals and progress data
  • Connected bank accounts (Plaid/Teller links are revoked)
  • Tax estimates and calculation history
  • AI conversation history
  • App preferences and settings

What Is Not Deleted

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to you
  • Records required by law to be retained (e.g., payment receipts for subscriptions)

Your bank account data is stored at Plaid / Teller, not in FlowMoney. Revoking access in FlowMoney removes our access token, but you may also want to remove FlowMoney directly in your bank's app or Plaid Portal at my.plaid.com.
