Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective: March 1, 2026

Short version: FlowMoney helps you track your money — not sell your data. We don't sell or share your personal information with advertisers. Ever.

1. Who We Are

FlowMoney ("we," "us," or "our") is a personal finance application designed for gig workers and self-employed individuals in the United States. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@flowmoney.app.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: email address, display name
  • Financial profile: average monthly income, work type (Uber, freelance, etc.), US state of residence
  • Manual transactions: cash income and expense entries you add yourself
  • Goals: savings goals and deadlines you set
  • Feedback: responses to in-app surveys you choose to complete

Information collected automatically:

  • App usage analytics: which screens you visit, feature interactions (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and error logs (no financial data included)
  • Device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility purposes

Bank connection data (optional):

If you choose to connect a bank account via Teller, we receive read-only transaction data from your financial institution. We do not store your bank credentials. Bank data is processed to display spending summaries and is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the App (income tracking, tax estimates, budget insights)
  • To calculate estimated self-employment taxes based on your state and income
  • To generate AI-powered financial insights (FlowAI feature)
  • To send optional weekly financial summaries and reminders (push notifications)
  • To improve the App based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your financial data for advertising targeting, credit scoring, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Financial profile data and transaction summaries are stored server-side to enable syncing across devices.

Manual cash transactions and certain preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and are not transmitted unless you are signed in.

We implement industry-standard security practices, but no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@flowmoney.app.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Teller — optional bank account connection (teller.io/legal/privacy)
  • RevenueCat — in-app subscription management (revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Expo / React Native — mobile app framework

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising networks.

6. Tax Calculations — Important Disclaimer

FlowMoney provides estimated tax calculations for informational purposes only. These estimates are based on 2025 IRS figures (Rev. Proc. 2024-40) and state rates from the Tax Foundation. They do not constitute professional tax advice.

Tax data provided by FlowMoney:

  • Assumes single filing status
  • Uses standard deduction ($15,000 for 2025)
  • Does not account for QBI deduction, credits, deductions, or prior-year payments
  • Uses top marginal rate for progressive states (actual liability may be lower)

Always consult a licensed CPA or tax professional for your actual tax obligations.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in FlowMoney
  • Correct inaccurate profile information at any time in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics tracking in Profile → Settings → Analytics
  • Disconnect your bank account at any time from the Dashboard
  • Export your data upon request (email us at privacy@flowmoney.app)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. EU/UK residents may have rights under GDPR. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

FlowMoney is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us immediately at privacy@flowmoney.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you in the App and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns:

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
