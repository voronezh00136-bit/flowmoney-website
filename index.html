What is the gig economy?

The gig economy is every person who earns money outside a traditional 9-to-5 job — no fixed salary, no employer-deducted taxes, no HR department to figure it out for you.

It's the Uber driver earning $2,800 this month and $1,100 next month. The Airbnb host juggling three listings. The freelance designer with six different clients. The personal trainer who gets paid cash after every session.

What they all share: no one automatically handles their taxes. The IRS still expects payment — they just don't make it easy. That's exactly the gap FlowMoney fills.
